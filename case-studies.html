Example walkthrough: AI Receptionist

AI receptionist for a dental clinic

This is an illustrative walkthrough of how a Clavyra AI receptionist handles a real enquiry end to end, built on the exact system we deploy for clinics. It is shared as an example of how the system works, not a completed client report.

The problem

Dental clinics lose patients to missed calls more than almost any other business. The front desk is busy assisting patients in the chair, the phone rings through to voicemail, and most callers hang up and call the next clinic on the list instead of leaving a message. After-hours calls, often from patients in pain, go completely unanswered until the next morning.

The current process, before automation

  • Patient calls during a procedure or after hours
  • Call goes unanswered or to voicemail
  • Front desk checks messages between patients, often hours later
  • By the time they call back, many patients have already booked elsewhere

If the caller mentions pain, swelling, or anything urgent, the AI skips booking and immediately flags the call to on-call staff for a callback, instead of treating it like a routine scheduling request.

Example AI instructions (simplified)

You are the AI receptionist for the clinic.
1. Greet the caller warmly and ask if they are a new or existing patient.
2. Ask the reason for the visit (checkup, pain, cleaning, emergency).
3. If the caller mentions pain, swelling, or bleeding, mark as urgent
   and notify on-call staff immediately. Do not attempt to book.
4. Otherwise, check calendar availability and offer the next 2-3
   open slots that match the patient's preference.
5. Confirm the booking, then send a WhatsApp/SMS confirmation
   and notify the front desk.

Appointment booking

Once confirmed, the appointment is written directly into the clinic's scheduling system in real time. The front desk sees the new booking appear on their calendar without lifting a finger, and the patient gets an instant confirmation message instead of waiting on a callback.

Expected business impact

Set up this way, a clinic can expect to capture after-hours and during-procedure calls that would otherwise go to voicemail, reduce no-shows through automatic confirmations, and free the front desk from constant phone interruptions so they can focus on the patients in front of them.

Real client: Website Development

Professional website for Romel Ventures

Romel Ventures is a venture consulting and ecosystem development firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, supporting founders and startups across Africa and emerging markets. They needed a professional website that clearly communicated their four core services and positioned them credibly to founders, accelerators, and corporate partners.

The brief

The existing online presence did not match the calibre of work and the clients Romel Ventures was working with. They needed a clean, fast, conversion-focused website that communicated expertise instantly, worked perfectly on mobile, and gave founders and partners a clear next step to engage.

What we built

  • Custom designed website showcasing all four service lines
  • Clear positioning for GTM consulting, venture readiness, ecosystem development, and AI advisory
  • Mobile-first, fast-loading design optimised for founders on the go
  • SEO optimised structure to rank for relevant consulting and startup ecosystem searches

The result

A professional, credible online presence that matches the quality of work Romel Ventures delivers. The site clearly communicates their expertise to the founders, accelerators, and corporate partners they work with across Africa and globally.

Real client: Website Development

Professional website for St Genevieve Academy

St Genevieve Academy is a school in Kenya. They needed a professional, mobile-friendly website that would let parents find information, make enquiries online, and build trust in the institution without needing to call for every question.

The brief

Parents were calling the school with basic questions that a well-structured website could answer automatically. The school needed a clean, fast, professional website that worked on any device and gave parents a clear way to reach out directly online.

What we built

  • Custom designed school website with clear information architecture
  • Mobile-first design for parents browsing on phones
  • Online enquiry and contact integration so parents could reach out directly
  • SEO optimised for local search so parents in the area could find the school
  • Fast loading on all connections including mobile data

"Since the new website went live, more parents are reaching out directly through the site instead of calling with basic questions. It is faster, looks professional, and works just as well on a phone as it does on a computer."

Lucy Mwangi, St Genevieve Academy
